2. Managing Templates

The main dashboard (/settings/templates) gives you a bird's-eye view of your document library.

Creating a New Template

  1. Click the large "New Template" card with the plus icon.
  2. A modal will appear asking for details:
    • Name: Give your template a clear, descriptive name (e.g., "Engineering Offer 2024").
    • Type: Select "Job", "Offer", or "Screening".
  3. Click "Create". You will be redirected to the specific editor for that template type.

Editing & Deleting

  • Edit: Simply click on any template card to open it in the editor.
  • Delete: Hover over a card and click the red Trash Icon in the top-left corner. Warning: This action cannot be undone.

3. Offer Template Builder

The Offer Template Builder is a powerful tool for creating professional offer letters. It uses a block-based system with rich text capabilities.

Key Features

  • Smart Blocks: Drag and drop pre-made sections like Opening, Compensation, Legal, and Signature from the left sidebar.
  • Custom Blocks: Need something unique? Add a "Custom Block" to write your own content.
  • Drag & Drop: Reorder sections on the canvas by dragging them up or down.
  • Remove Blocks: Drag a block back to the sidebar (it turns red) or click the trash icon on the block itself to remove it.

Using Variables

Make your templates dynamic using Variables. These are placeholders that get replaced with real data when you send an offer.

How to use: Place your cursor in the text editor, then click a variable chip (e.g., + Candidate Name) in the toolbar.

Common Variables:

  • {{candidate_name}} - The candidate's full name.
  • {{job_title}} - The role they are being hired for.
  • {{salary}} - The annual base salary.
  • {{start_date}} - Their first day of work.

Previewing

Click the "Preview Full" button in the header to see a full-page preview of your offer letter. This generates a realistic view (PDF/Email style) with dummy data, so you can check formatting and layout.

4. Job Template Builder

Similar to the Offer Builder, the Job Template Builder helps you structure job descriptions.

  • AI Assistance: Use AI blocks to automatically generate "Responsibilities" or "Requirements" based on the job title.
  • Structure: Define the standard sections every job post should have to ensure consistency across your careers page.
