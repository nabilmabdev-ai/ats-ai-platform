üåä Application Workflows

Recruiter Workflow: From Job Creation to Hire

  1. Create Job: A recruiter starts by creating a new job, either from scratch or using a template. The AI generates a full job description. The job is then set to PENDING_APPROVAL.
  2. Approval: A hiring manager receives a notification, reviews the job details, and approves it. The job status changes to PUBLISHED.
  3. Candidate Inflow: As candidates apply, their resumes are automatically parsed and scored. They appear in the "Applied" column of the Kanban board. Candidates failing knockout questions are auto-rejected.
  4. Screening: The recruiter reviews candidates in the "Applied" column, using the AI Score and Summary to quickly identify top talent. They drag promising candidates to the "Screening" stage.
  5. Interviewing: After a phone screen, the recruiter moves the candidate to "Interview". They use the Smart Scheduler to coordinate times with the interview team and send a booking link to the candidate.
  6. Offer: Post-interview, the team decides to make an offer. The recruiter uses the Offer Generator to create a PDF from a template and sends it for e-signature. The candidate card is moved to the "Offer" stage.
  7. Hired: The candidate signs the offer. The recruiter receives a notification and drags the candidate to the "Hired" column. The workflow is complete.

Candidate Workflow: The Application Experience

  1. Find Job: The candidate discovers the job posting on the's career page or a job board.
  2. Apply: The candidate fills out a simple application form, answers knockout questions, and uploads their PDF resume.
  3. Confirmation: The candidate receives an automated email confirming their application has been received.
  4. Scheduling: If selected for an interview, the candidate receives an email with a booking link to choose a time slot that works for them.
  5. Offer Notification: If successful, the candidate receives an email with a secure link to view and e-sign their offer letter.

Hiring Manager/Interviewer Workflow: Review and Feedback

  1. Review Candidates: The hiring manager can view all candidates for their job, read AI summaries, and leave comments for the recruiter.
  2. Interview Preparation: Before an interview, the interviewer receives a calendar invite with a link to the "Dedicated Interview Mode" page, which shows the candidate's resume, skills, and the structured scorecard.
  3. Provide Feedback: During or after the call, the interviewer fills out the scorecard and their raw notes. The AI Copilot then processes the notes to generate a structured summary, pros, and cons.
  4. Final Decision: The hiring manager and recruiter review all feedback and scorecards in one place to make a final hiring decision.
